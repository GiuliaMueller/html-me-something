Hope's Slide Rule

This story about my niece, Hope, is a few years old.

When she was in she would love to tell me new things she learned in school. One day she came home and told me that she learned the Slide Rule.

Color me impressed! I never learned that archaic skill when I was in school, and she was learning it in kindergarten! I asked her to explain it, and she said,

Only one kid on the slide at a time.

So much for math!

This story is adapted from an article I read in Reader's Digest®. Though not true for my family, it is a true story titled "Playground Rules".
